Who we are

Chapter 84 is an active Special Forces Association chapter in Hampton Roads, Virginia. We support our members, honor our fallen, and strengthen our community ties.

What we do

  • Support to JROTC and ROTC programs
  • Participation in regional events and ceremonies
  • Networking and mutual support for members
